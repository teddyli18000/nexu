# WhatsApp

Just scan a QR code with your phone to connect your personal WhatsApp to nexu — takes less than 2 minutes.

## Step 1: Choose the WhatsApp channel

Open the nexu client and click **WhatsApp** in the "Choose a channel to get started" section.

![Choose WhatsApp channel](/assets/whatsapp/step1-choose-whatsapp.webp)

## Step 2: Scan the QR code

1. In the "Connect WhatsApp" dialog, click the "Scan WhatsApp QR" button.

![Click Scan WhatsApp QR](/assets/whatsapp/step2-scan-qr-button.webp)

2. nexu will generate a QR code and show "Waiting for WhatsApp scan".

![Waiting for scan](/assets/whatsapp/step2-waiting-scan.webp)

3. On your phone, open **WhatsApp**, tap the "Yourself" tab at the bottom, then tap the QR code icon in the top-right corner.

![Tap QR code icon on phone](/assets/whatsapp/step3-phone-settings.webp)

4. On the QR code page, tap the "Scan" button at the bottom.

![Tap Scan button](/assets/whatsapp/step3-phone-scan-button.webp)

5. Point your phone at the QR code on your computer screen. Once scanned, tap "Confirm" to complete the link.

![Confirm linking](/assets/whatsapp/step3-phone-confirm.webp)

## Step 3: Start chatting

Once the QR code is scanned, the WhatsApp channel will show as connected. Click "Chat" to jump to WhatsApp and start talking to your Agent 🎉

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need a public server?**

No. nexu connects via the WhatsApp Web protocol — no public IP or callback URL required.

**Q: Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?**

No. A personal WhatsApp account works just fine.

**Q: Will this affect my normal WhatsApp usage?**

No. nexu connects as a linked device, exactly like using WhatsApp Web on a computer. Your phone continues to work normally.

**Q: What if my computer is off — can the Agent still reply?**

The nexu client needs to be running. As long as nexu is running in the background (and your computer isn't asleep), the Agent will be online 24/7 to reply to WhatsApp messages.
